Block of the Wilds (Fall 2024)

A narrative of the day-to-day life of a Minecraft player as they travel throughout their vibrant world. The footage was recorded in-game using the Replay Mod feature and edited in Premiere Pro, experimenting with camera shots and adding ambient sounds.

Endless Adventures (Fall 2024)

A showcase of my adventures in modded Minecraft and with friends in real-life. The Minecraft footage was recorded in-game using the Replay Mod feature and real-life footage using my phone, together edited in Premiere Pro. The video is a love-letter to the game as a source of nostalgia and memories it has created, and to my friends for all the experiences we shared.

Adventures with Cattie (Spring 2024)

Features the random adventures and antics of a small cat named Cattie. Made using Photoshop and edited in After Effects, this video was a fun project to practice camera tracking and integrating art with captured footage.

Hallows Night (Spring 2024)

A story of me and my friend trick-or-treating on a fateful Halloween night. The footage was created in Photoshop and edited in After Effects, and was created as a fun skit with me and my friend dressed as our favorite video game characters!

Anticipation (Spring 2024)

A short video of me and my friends waiting for the fabled DVD screensaver to hit the corner of the screen. Created in Photoshop and animated in After Effects, this video was to practice motion and keyframing while also making a funny story.

Anomalous Encounter (Spring 2024)

Found footage of an anomalous entity within my laptop, inspired by an online horror series. Recorded on phone and edited in Photoshop and After Effects. This video was a way to practice masking and camera tracking, and I had fun making it themed around my favorite digital and psychological horror media.

Navi Flight (Spring 2024)

A short animation of Navi the fairy from the Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time. The model and animation was made using Blender, and served as a fun introduction to the software.
