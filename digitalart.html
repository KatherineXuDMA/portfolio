Floral Gates (2024)

A quaint flower swing that serves as a gateway to a floral paradise. This piece was created in Photoshop, experimenting with filters and color adjustments to create a warm and bright environment, highlighted by the pink of the flowers.

Frog Pond (2024)

A shy little frog resting over a colorful koi pond in the spring. Made in Photoshop and continued to play around with more adjustments related to coloring and filters, creating a vibrant pond filled with flowers and a very happy froggie.

Cats on Bubbles (2024)

Creation of Donut (2024)

The beautiful reunion of a cat and their beloved blender donut. As my first blender project, I created the iconic donut model and decided to give it a little sparkly flare in Photoshop as well! This was a fun introductory practice in Blender and was a lot of fun to make.

Archaic Hallways (2024)

Created in Photoshop, this piece was another experiment on colors adjustments, filters, and also object selection. I wanted to create a surreal and abstract environent, combining various images and giving each unique colors and filters. The finished work resembles much of the dreamcore or weirdcore internet aesthetic.

Imperfect Place (2024)

This piece was inspired by a horror game themed around liminal spaces and the popular Backrooms concept. I made this in Illustrator and Photoshop and wanted to creat a sense of dread, abandonment, and lonliness as a lone figure wanders through an empty city with nothing but a flickering billboard affirming his worst fears.

Suffering Leaves Suffering Leaves (2024)

This piece, created in Photoshop, was themed around pain and suffering as a grotesque, living tree pierces out of the figures body, causing them to bleed and feed the tree even more in a continous cycle of blood and growth. The name of this project was inspired by a video game with a similar premise of blood called Ultrakill.

Beyond Yourself (2024)

This piece presents a man standing before an abstract, incomprehensible being made of flowers and tendrils, creating a sense of eldritch horror. Most of the objects used in this project were taken off of my phone around campus, selecting and combining them into this biblical horror-themed piece.

Red Woods (2024)

This project was also created to follow the theme of dread and abandonment. A little girl is lost in a forest at night, with ominous shadows and whispers that tell her that she won't ever leave the endless woods.
