Floral Gates (Spring 2024)

A quaint flower swing that serves as a gateway to a floral paradise. This piece was created in Photoshop, experimenting with filters and color adjustments to create a warm and bright environment, highlighted by pink flowers.

Frog Pond (Spring 2024)

A shy little frog resting along a colorful koi pond in the spring. Made in Photoshop and made to play around with more adjustments related to coloring and filters, creating a vibrant pond filled with flowers and a very happy froggie.

Cats on Bubbles (Spring 2024)

A content and happy cat floating on some colorful bubbles. This project was created in Blender as an introduction to lighting and reflections, and the cat was added in afterwards in Photoshop as a finishing touch. It was fun experimenting with coloring and I think bubbles were a perfect candidate for that.

Creation of Donut (Spring 2024)

The beautiful reunion of a cat and their beloved blender donut. As my first blender project, I created the iconic donut model and decided to give it a little sparkly flare in Photoshop! This was a introductory practice in Blender and was a lot of fun to make.

Archaic Hallways (Spring 2024)

Created in Photoshop, this piece was another experiment on colors adjustments, filters, and also object selection. I wanted to create a surreal and abstract environent, combining various images and giving each unique colors and filters. The finished work resembles much of the dreamcore or weirdcore internet aesthetic.

Imperfect Place (Spring 2024)

This piece was inspired by a horror game themed around liminal spaces and the popular Backrooms concept. I made this in Illustrator and Photoshop and wanted to create a sense of dread, abandonment, and loneliness as a lone figure wanders through an empty city with nothing but a flickering billboard affirming his worst fears.

Suffering Leaves Suffering Leaves (Spring 2024)

Created in Photoshop, this pice was themed around pain and suffering as a grotesque, living tree pierces out of the figure's body, causing them to bleed and feed the tree even more in a continous cycle of blood and growth. The name of this project was inspired by a video game with a similar premise of blood as fuel called Ultrakill.

Beyond Yourself (Spring 2024)

This piece presents a man standing before an abstract, incomprehensible being made of flowers and tendrils, creating a sense of eldritch horror. Most of the objects used in this project were taken using my phone around campus, selecting and combining them into this biblical horror-themed piece.

Red Woods (Spring 2024)

This project was also created with theme of dread and abandonment, featuring a little girl lost in a forest at night, with ominous shadows and whispers that tell her that she won't ever leave the endless woods.
